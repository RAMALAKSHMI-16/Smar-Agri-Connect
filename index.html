About Smart Agri Connect

Smart Agri Connect is a platform dedicated to solving the market access problem faced by village farmers. We eliminate middlemen, connect farmers directly with buyers, and ensure fair prices for high-quality produce.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where farmers can thrive while consumers get access to fresh, locally grown products.
